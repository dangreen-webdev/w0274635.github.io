My Goals

Short Term Goals:

My main goal over the next year is to find a work placement for this upcoming summer. A requirement of the IT Web Development program is we are required to find an IT business or company to work for a term and gain some experience of what it is like to work in the industry. I am hoping to find a good solid place to work to start my career off on the right path.

I would like to work on my networking skills over the next while and try and meet more people in the industry and get my name out there. I have been told by multiple people who have already had success in the IT industry that networking is a crucial part in landing a good position.

Getting involved in some of the extra programs at school is another thing that I have been planning on getting involved in. There are lots of different things I could get involved in around the school that I could benefit from and it would look great on a resume.

Another goal of mine is to become better at client side programming. So far I have been mostly writing programs and running them through Node, but I would like to eventually be able to fully integrate Javascript into my web pages to make them do cool things.

Lastly, I would like to learn the fundamentals of as many coding languages as possible over the next year or so. I find learning new coding languages very exciting. It would give me the ability to do more things with my projects.
